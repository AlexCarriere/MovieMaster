The Grinch





Rating: PG 13
Length: 1h 26m
Director(s): Yarrow Cheney, and Scott Mosier
Featuring: Benedict Cumberbatch, Cameron Seely, and Rashida Jones
In the town of Whoville, human-like creatures called Whos are filled with excitement about celebrating Christmas. The only one who isn't amused is a cantankerous, green furred creature called The Grinch, who has a heart "two sizes too small" and lives in a cave on the top of Mount Crumpit just north of Whoville. The Grinch's only friend is his loyal pet dog Max and they both only visit Whoville when the Grinch needs to buy food and do bad things.
